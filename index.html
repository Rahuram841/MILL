ROTARACT CLUB OF COIMBATORE MILLENNIUM

Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Millennium is a community-based club belonging to the Rotary International District 3201.

RaC Coimbatore Millennium is parented by Rotary Club of Coimbatore Millennium and enjoys patronage and enormous support from the parenting Rotary. The Club was Chartered on 12th October,2001.

Mission

Actions remain a Rotaractors identity and for the Rotaractors of Coimbatore Millennium the projects-Spotlight, Speak-up, Atom, Asgard, Go Gold and Giving Hope remain their identity over ages. The club is known for building friendship with clubs intra-district and inter-district. To sum up in a phrase, RaC Coimbatore Millennium is a pack of people with diverse talents, ideas, and style of working who possess the common spirit of Rotaraction and friendship.
